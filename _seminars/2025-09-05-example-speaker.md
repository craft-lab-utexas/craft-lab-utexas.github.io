title: "Autoformalization of Graduate-Level Mathematics Using Large Language Models"
abstract: >
  In this talk we explore recent advances in autoformalization — the process of 
  encoding natural language mathematical statements into machine-verifiable formal 
  proofs. We present a new approach leveraging large language models fine-tuned on 
  the Mathlib4 library, achieving state-of-the-art results on a benchmark of 
  graduate-level theorems.
