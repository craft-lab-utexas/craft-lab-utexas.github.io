## Working With Us

CRaFT Lab is interested in collaborating with students, faculty and staff researchers 
working in the areas of Formal Methods, AI, Mathematics/CS theory and/or scalable systems. We welcome inquiries from prospective researchers with backgrounds in one or more of the following areas:

- Formal Methods, including Interactive Theorem Provers (e.g., Lean), SAT/SMT solvers, 
  or model checking
- AI/ML, particularly as applied to reasoning, formalization, or verification
- Systems and infrastructure for research computing
- Theoretical Mathematics, Computer Science, or Logic. 

If you are interested in contacting CRaFT Lab, please send an email to 
[jstubbs@tacc.utexas.edu](mailto:jstubbs@tacc.utexas.edu). 

We anticipate having funding for one or more positions in the near future. 
We will post specific funded position announcements below as they become available.
