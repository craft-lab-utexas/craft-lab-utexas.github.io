CRaFT Lab develops and hosts production-grade, open software infrastructure 
for formal reasoning and AI. A core part of our mission is making powerful 
tools genuinely accessible to researchers and educators.

Our tools and services leverage world-class computing infrastructure at the [Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC)](https://tacc.utexas.edu).

## Access

CRaFT Lab tools and services are intended to be open and accessible to the 
UT Austin research community and beyond. If you are interested in early access 
or would like to discuss a specific use case, please contact 
[jstubbs@tacc.utexas.edu](mailto:jstubbs@tacc.utexas.edu).

## Contributing

CRaFT Lab is committed to open source development. Our tools and infrastructure 
code will be available on [GitHub](https://github.com/provables/). 
Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are welcome.
